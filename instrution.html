  • The total duration of the examination is 60 minutes.
  • The clock is synchronized with the server. The countdown timer, located at the top right corner of the screen, will display the remaining time available to complete the examination. Once the timer reaches zero, the examination will automatically end. You will not be required to manually submit your examination.
  • The Question Palette, displayed on the right side of the screen, will indicate the status of each question using specific symbols.
    - You have selected an answer.
    - No answer selected.
    - You have answered the question
    - Answered but flagged for review.
    - You chose an answer but want to review it later.

    • The Marked for Review status for a question simply shows that you want to review it again. If a question is answered and marked for review, your response will be included in the evaluation.
